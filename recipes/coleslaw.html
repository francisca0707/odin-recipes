Coleslaw

Description

coleslaw is an amazing dish that can be eaten on its own, used as a side dish or can be used to garnish some dishes eg jollof rice

Ingredients

  • cabbage
  • carrot
  • cucumber
  • mayonaise

Steps

  1. wash the vegetables
  2. grate them with a grater
  3. mix them thoroughly together
  4. add the mayonaise
  5. surprise! our coleslaw is ready in less than a jiffy!
