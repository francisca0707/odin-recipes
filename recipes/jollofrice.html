Jollof Rice

Description

Smoking hot jollof rice, hmmm yummy! This recipe is very popular and delicious, the best part is that it is easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • water
  • vegetable oil
  • salt
  • seasoning cubes
  • onions
  • fresh pepper
  • fresh tomatoes
  • curry and thyne

Steps

  1. put a pot of water on the stove and bring the water to the boil
  2. add the rice to the boiling water
  3. allow to boil for 5 minutes and drain the excess water
  4. put an empty pot on the stove and add some vegetable oil
  5. slice the onions and add to the oil
  6. blend the tomato and pepper and also add to the oil, fry the mixture for like 5 minutes
  7. add enough water to cook the rice
  8. add the rice and the rest of the ingredients, cover the pot and cook till almost all the water dries up
  9. stir the rice and turn the heat off
  10. garnish with coleslaw and I bet you, you cant resist the yumminess!
