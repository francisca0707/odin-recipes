Yam Porridge

Description

Yam porridge is a delicacy that is especially simple to make and very enjoyable indeed.

Ingredients

  • yam
  • water
  • salt
  • pepper
  • palm oil
  • seasoning cubes
  • crayfish

Steps

  1. peel yam and cut into cubes
  2. wash and put in pot and add water to the level of yam in the pot
  3. put the pot on the stove and bring to boil
  4. add the rest of the ingredients and cook till the yam is tender
  5. serve hot
